INSTRUCTIONS
Start Game:
1. Put attendance in above
2. Select a goalie from the "Lineup Structure" dropdown below
3. Press "Create Lineup Chart"
3. This will create a list of positions & players. Tell the players their positions
4. Press "Add Starting Lineup"
The game has begun!

6 Minutes Into Quarter:
1. Before the sub tell the players who are going on (the ones in the dropdowns) where they are going
2. When you can sub, call off the players listed under the buttons and tell the players on the sideline to go on the field
3. Once the game has resumed, press "Process Substitution" to save the substitutions

At Quarter End:
1. Press "Clear Suggestions"
2. Select a new goalie with the Goalie dropdown
3. Press "Hotswap"
4. Gather all the players and tell them their new positions (these are the ones in the dropdowns!)
5. Once the new set of players is on the field, click "Process Substitution" to save the substitution
Now... go back to the steps for the 6 minute sub to continue for the next quarter
